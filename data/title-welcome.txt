Welcome to the Lucy the Diamond Girl.

Collect diamonds to open exit
to the next level.

Don't touch radioactive animals,
they will kill you.

Falling boulders and diamonds
will also kill you.

Controls:
 * cursor keys to move
 * shift+cursor keys to manipulate
   without moving
 * escape key to quit
 * F1 for help
 * F2 for legend
 * ALT+ENTER toggle windowed/fullscreen
 * PAUSE to pause
